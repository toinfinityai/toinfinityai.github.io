RepSet

A synthetic open-source dataset for remote fitness and remote PT applications. Includes videos of a single avatar doing multiple reps of common exercises with a rich set of labels and annotations, including rep counts.

OVERVIEW

Sophisticated graphics meets deep learning

RepSet aims to promote and advance research and product development in remote exercise and computer vision.

It is the first video dataset for human pose estimation and one of the few fitness datasets that is available for both academic and commercial use.

RepSet highlights the Infinity product’s ability to generate infinite movement variation and perform each rep slightly differently, just like real humans. The videos are accompanied by a rich set of labels and annotations, including rep counts.


It features:

  • 1,000 videos of 10-20s each
  • 10 exercises
  • 50 indoor scenes
  • Full spectrum of lighting conditions
  • Varied avatar body shape, skin tones, and clothing
  • 25 label and annotation types
