Data on demand

The Infinity API empowers ML engineers to generate the exact data they need at the click of a button. Explore the two fitness products below: VisionFit and SenseFit.

VisionFit

Labeled exercise videos

The VisionFit API allows users to generate synthetic videos of a variety of exercises. Users choose the number of reps, camera location, environmental conditions, and more. Every video comes with pixel-perfect labels, including semantic segmentation masks, rep counts, occlusion, and 3D keypoints!

SenseFit

Labeled wearables data

The SenseFit API allows users to generate time-series wearables data for a variety of exercises. Simulate Apple Watch data or other IMU sensor (angular position) data at any body location. Every dataset comes with continuous rep count labels and other metadata.
