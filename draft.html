Infinite Possibilities

Generating synthetic data leapfrogs the traditional data collection and labeling process.

Enables rapid iteration

Hypotheses can be tested using synthetic images instead of waiting for real-world data. This enables tailoring of models to specific applications and can result in better model performance.

Rapid iteration enabled our team to build a pose estimation model that outperformed Google's BlazePose model!

Check out the results

Provides unlimited labels

Infinity AI synthetic data comes with pixel-perfect labels that might be hard or even impossible to get via manual annotations. Labels can include semantic segmentation, 2D and 3D keypoints, bounding boxes, 3D cuboids, depth, surface normals, camera position, objects attributes (e.g. human body dimensions), and more.

Minimizes data bias

Infinite AI synthetic data enables complete control over distributions of data, including body shape, skin tone, and presenting gender, ensuring models with minimal bias.

Our open source dataset for remote fitness applications, InfiniteForm, was born out of our goal to make remote fitness a more inclusive experience thaat works accurately across all body shapes and skin tones.
